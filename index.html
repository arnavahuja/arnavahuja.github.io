About Me

Who Am I?

Hey, I'm Arnav Ahuja. I am a third-year student at Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, India majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics (dual degree). My passion lies in the intersection of technology, math and business and I find Data Science to be the perfect blend of those interests. I believe in the tremendous, yet untapped potential of this field. My quest for knowledge propels me to learn more, train better and explore deeper into the science of Machine Learning.

My undergraduate degree has introduced me to various Data Science techniques and also helped me understand the statistics behind the algorithms. Over the last year, I have been exploring different algorithms in Computer Vision and Deep Learning and strengthening my skills through research projects and internships. I’m a creative innovator and problem-solver, eager to thrive by exploring opportunities and learn quickly in the environment which fosters a culture of innovation, collaboration and hard work. 

When I am not geeking out, I play tennis and swim. I also volunteer at an NGO where I tutor high school students. Other things that intrigue me are novels, movies and travelling.
